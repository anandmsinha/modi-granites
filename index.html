Who we are

Modi Granites Pvt. Ltd., Established in the year 2012 is a young company by age, but vastly experienced in the granite field through its management. The promoters of Modi Granites Pvt. Ltd. have been associated with the industry for more than 20 years.

The primary business of the promoters is that of supplying material handling equipment and the Granite industry is one of their leading clientele. The promoters have befriended various mine owners and industrialists and have access to continuous supplies of various granite colors. The company, in the near future, intends to backward integrate and have its own quarries as well.

The company today comprises of highly experienced and trained staff who not only ensure the best quality of product, but also promise to deliver every commitment with impeccable efficiency and supreme satisfaction. We believe in doing business, but doing so rightly is more important and hence we intend to carry a smile always and ensure everybody associated with our company does so too.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to be one of the leading granite slab processing units in India and one of the most preferred company to supply granite across the world.

Our Mission: We want to achieve our vision by providing the best quality of stone at the best price with an attitude of exceptional client service making the entire journey of doing business pleasurable not only to our clients but to everybody associated with our company.
